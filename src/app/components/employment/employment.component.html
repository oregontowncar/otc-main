Global Chauffeur Network

Here at Oregon Town car, we take pride in our ability to deliver the best service when managing your transportation needs throughout your journey. Our services are available in over seven hundred cities worldwide, making us excellent in arranging smooth business and leisure trips.

Chauffeurs

Employment Type: Full Time / Part Time.

Description
  • Provide safe operation of vehicles and comply with all motor vehicle regulations and traffic laws.
  • Provide timely pick-up and drop-off of our valued customers.
  • Maintain the appearance of vehicles while on duty.
  • Display and maintain a professional, personal appearance by total compliance with the dress code.
  • Communicate with dispatchers and sales team to promote and maintain workflow/customer satisfaction.
Qualifications
  • Excellent communication skills.
  • Commitment to providing exceptional customer service.
  • Pleasant, friendly, helpful demeanor.
  • Ability to multi-task.
  • Effective Time Management Skills.
  • Valid Driver’s License; for larger vehicles CDL B preferred with passenger endorsement & DOT card.
  • High school diploma or GED equivalent preferred.
  • Punctual and reliable attendance.
  • Industry background preferred.
  • Strong knowledge of mapping programs.
  • Minimum age of 23 and clean driving record.

All employees must pass a background check and drug test.

Dispatchers

Employment Type: Full Time / Part Time.

Description
  • Take customer and affiliate reservations from a multitude of communications channels including, email, telephone, and internet-based distribution systems.
  • Dispatch and scheduling limousine, van, bus, and other vehicle trips.
  • Confirm Driver availability.
  • Address customer concerns and service emergencies with urgency and care.
  • Maintain a clean, concise log of daily activities covering service levels, incidents, and vehicle maintenance status.
  • Conduct regular, consistent data entry and follow-up with attention to detail.
  • Communicate with drivers and sales team to promote and maintain workflow and customer satisfaction.
Qualifications
  • High School Diploma or GED equivalent or 2 years or more related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience.
  • Knowledge and practice of safety rules and procedures are required.

All employees must pass a background check and drug test.
